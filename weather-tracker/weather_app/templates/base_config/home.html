Welcome to the Weather Tracker

You can view the following endpoints:

  • Weather App UI
  • Weather for city : "http://127.0.0.1:8000/weather/<city>"
  • Weather for city in preferred language (EN/DE/FR): "http://127.0.0.1:8000/weather/<city>/<lang>"
  • Weather for city in preferred language (EN/DE/FR) and cache timeout (5/10/60) in minutes: "http://127.0.0.1:8000/weather/<city>/<lang><cache_timeout>"
